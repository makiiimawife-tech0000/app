💰 WHAT IS ARBITRAGE?
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

When YES + NO < $1.00, you profit by:
  1. Buy YES share
  2. Buy NO share  
  3. Merge → Get $1.00
  4. Keep the difference!

Example:
  YES: $0.47
  NO:  $0.50
  ─────────
  Cost: $0.97
  Get:  $1.00
  Profit: $0.03 (minus fees)
